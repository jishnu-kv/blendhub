The privacy policy describes how, what and why some data might be required to be collected. By using BlendHub, you acknowledge and consent to the practices described below.

**User data**
This application doesn't store, collect, use or share any personal/analytics data. All user data including project information, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device in the AppData\Roaming\BlendHub folder.

**Internet connection**
This app requires internet connection only for the following features:
* Downloading Blender versions from blender.org
* Fetching available Blender version information

You can use the app without an internet connection but the download features will not work properly.

**Other websites**
Some features may contain links to other websites (such as blender.org or GitHub) that are not operated by the developer. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. You are strongly advised to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

**Changes**
The privacy policy may suffer changes from time to time to reflect updates made to the app. When this Policy is changed in a material manner, you'll be informed by updating the "Last update" section.
